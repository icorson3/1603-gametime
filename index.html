Welcome to 2018!
The objective of the game is simple.
Get a single tile to 2048 and you win.
In order to do this, us the arrow keys
to push tiles to their respective sides
of the board. If a tile collides with another
tile of the same value, one will double and
the other will disappear. Be careful as a
new tile is also created each time you move!
